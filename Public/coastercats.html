Coaster Cats at the University of Arizona
About:
The Theme Park Entertainment Group at the University of Arizona also known as Coaster Cats brings engineering and art together to design rollercoasters. We strive to connect students across many disciplines to collaborate on projects and learn about the theme park industry. We attend competitions and conferences in order to apply our efforts in the club.
